ZUBE Robotics

“ZUBE Robotics strives to improve the quality of life of consumers and make robots universally accessible”

Beginnings

ZUBE Robotics was established when 3 high school friends had a seed of an idea that sprung into a tree. Since its inception, ZUBE Robotics has strived to introduce friendly companion robots to all spectrums of society from home to industry. The founders of ZUBE Robotics hope that each home, business and school will have a ZUBE robot and that the robots will increase the society’s standard of living.

The original ZUBE Concept created by Ammar as a young child.

A Global Reach

Although the company is headquartered in Carrollton, Texas, ZUBE Robotics serves people around the world. ZUBE Robotics consistently receives positive feedback from consumers of all walks of life including teachers, doctors, and first-responders. Because of this positive feedback, ZUBE Robotics is the global market leader for the development, manufacturing, and marketing of robots.
