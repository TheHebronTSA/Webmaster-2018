STEM @ HEBRON

Science Cources at Hebron

AP Chemistry

This 36 week advanced-level course is designed to provide a comprehensive first-year college-level chemistry experience. Emphasis is placed on preparation for the Advanced Placement Chemistry exam. In addition, students partake in labs and other related activities that provide a further understanding.

Medical Terminology

This course is a Latin and Greek language-based class that studies the structure of medical terminology. Employability skills are emphasized. This is a fast-paced class that encourages collaborative, as well as independent learning. Requires good attendance and a motivated learner.

Anatomy and Physiology

A study of the structure and function of human anatomy, including the neuroendocrine, integumentary, and musculoskeletal, digestive, urinary, reproductive, respiratory, and circulatory systems. Content may be either integrated or specialized.

Medical Microbiology

This course offers an in-depth study of viruses, bacteria, fungi, and protists. Emphasis is on the knowledge of relationships between microbes and health maintenance and the role of microbes in infectious disease.

Pathophysiology

This course offers a study in the mechanisms and processes of pathology. Emphasis will be placed on knowing a variety of human diseases and the effects of disease prevention and control.
