AI in Today's World

Summary

The use of AI has seen a recent uptake in the business world. On a practical level, Uber has employed self driving cars in many of the nations cities. These cars require no driver to constantly turn the wheel or adjust any lights, they just go on their own. Similarly, Amazon has also been marketing a smart home speaker called the Amazon Echo with the personal assistant Alexa. Currently, the speaker can do tasks such as setting a timer or changing the music playing in a room. Another company that uses AI is Intel. In many of their processors, with the assistance of integrated AI, Intel is able to make the processors optimize themselves and conduct the processes at a more efficient rate. The AI that video games offer are also an interesting concept. Whether it is a simple tic tac toe or something more complex, video game AI offers varying difficulty and an interesting element to these games.

Google Assistant

Google Assistant was a personal assistant introduced by google in 2016. The app was initially available for Google devices and eventually introduced to Apple devices. Tasks such as setting timers, searching the internet, and scheduling events can be completed by the device. The Google Assistant is able to converse with the user which assists with communication. An app as well as a home speaker is available for use.

Uber Self-Driving Cars

Self-driving cars are vehicles that can assess the environment and drive on its on own. Many companies have attempted to use the vehicle and the most notable being Uber. Uber has sent out the car to many cities in the United States as part of their arsenal of taxis. Generally autonomous vehicles have a lot of sensory equipment, Uber’s self driving cars are no different. With multiple sensors on the hood and inside the car, the automobile can effectively and safely traverse through many roads.

Pandora

Pandora is one of the most revolutionary techs that exists out there today. The Pandora software uses subsriber preferences and listening history to put together a playlist for the listeners. The software is based on 400 musical characteristics and the system has an incredible track record for recommending songs that would otherwise go unnoticed by the people they are paired with.
